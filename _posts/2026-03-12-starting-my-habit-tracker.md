Today I'm starting work on my habit tracker project. The goal is to build a local-first app that helps track daily habits using AI-assisted insights.

In this post I'll document why I'm building it, what problem it solves, and how I plan to approach the architecture.

## Why a Habit Tracker?

Tracking habits manually is tedious. I want something that:
- Runs locally (no cloud dependency)
- Uses AI to provide smart reminders and insights
- Is simple and fast to use

## Initial Plan

I'll start with a minimal MVP — just logging habits and viewing streaks. Then I'll layer in LangGraph for the AI workflow orchestration.
